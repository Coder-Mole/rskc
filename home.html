Welcome to the Rossendale Shotokan Karate Club

Established 30 years ago, Rossendale SKC is one of the most well-established and highly reputed Karate Clubs in the Rossendale Valley, winner of Rossendale Leisure trusts community club of the year 2014. we operate three Dojos in the area, one in Haslingden, Bacup and Whitworth.

Chief Instructor Stephen Warburton has been a student of the club and over 16 years of hard training has seen him rise to the position of Chief Instructor holding the rank of 3rd Dan, along with his panel of senior instructors, the club has an accumulated wealth of knowledge and experience of over 60 years.

Rossendale SKC is affiliated to the Karate Union of Great Britain (KUGB), the largest Shotokan organisation in the country. The KUGB is a body of over 300 Karate clubs, all of which practice Shotokan Karate. It was established in 1966 as a democratic and non-profit making organisation for the development of Shotokan Karate and is affiliated to European and World Shotokan Karate Bodies.


About the Club

We are a very family-friendly, committee run, traditional Shotokan karate club providing training.

We also run a special Tiger Cubs for 4 - 6yr olds, focusing on fun, body coordination, manners and discipline

Parents are very welcome to train alongside their children and train for free encouraging whole families to train and bond together.

The benefits of Karate training include (although not limited to): Self-Discipline, Coordination, Concentration, Focus of Mind & Body, Respect (for self as well as for others), Improved Health & Fitness, Self-Defence, Confidence.


The club has excellent contacts with local schools, Sensei Warburton regularly teaches special courses after schools to pupils of all ages, if you’re interested please contact your school to arrange a meeting.

Tuition costs are very affordable, and if you are not sure whether Karate is for you then why not csample a few classes? The first three sessions are free so don’t hesitate to come down and give it a go.

So, if you're looking for a friendly, professionally run, traditional karate club in Rossendale, why not try us first at the Rossendale Shotokan Karate Club?
